# Privacy Policy
Effective Date: 5 November 2025
Website: 25565.club – https://25565.club/

25565.club (“we,” “our,” or “us”) values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you create an account and use our services, including custom subdomains.

1. Eligibility
Our services are intended for users who are at least 13 years old. By using our services, you confirm that you meet this age requirement. EU users under the local legal age (16 in most countries) must obtain parental consent.

2. Information We Collect
When you create an account and use our services, we may collect:
- Account Information: Username and password. **Do not use your real name** as your username.
- Subdomain Data: Content and settings associated with your custom subdomains.
- Usage Data: Anonymous technical information such as IP address (immediately anonymized), browser type, and device information through Databuddy.
- No cookies are used, and no cookie consent is required.

3. How We Use Your Information
We use your information to:
- Create and manage your user account.
- Enable and maintain your custom subdomains.
- Provide technical support and troubleshoot issues.
- Improve and maintain the website and services.
- Communicate important updates about your account or our services.

4. Data Storage and Security
We use Appwrite (self-hosted) for our API and Databuddy for anonymous session tracking. We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. No system can guarantee 100% security.

5. Sharing Your Information
We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data:
- With service providers (Appwrite and Databuddy) solely to provide the services.
- If required by law or to protect our rights or safety.

6. Third-Party Services
Databuddy handles only anonymous session data. Appwrite handles account and subdomain data. Review their documentation for details.

7. Your Rights
Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including:
- Accessing, correcting, or deleting your data.
- Requesting account deletion.

To exercise these rights, contact us at: admin@25565.club

8. Data Retention
We retain your account and subdomain data as long as your account is active or as necessary to provide our services.

9. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Effective Date” at the top indicates the last update.
