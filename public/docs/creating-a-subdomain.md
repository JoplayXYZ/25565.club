# Creating a subdomain

This page explains how to create a subdomain with 25565.club and does not cover [how to create and manage dns records](/docs?page=dns-records) with that said here is how to create a subdomain.

<br>

## Step 1:

[Sign](/auth/login) into your 25565.club account or [create an account](/auth/register) if you don't have one. Then open your [dashboard](/dash).

## Step 2:

Once on your dashboard click on "Add a subdomain"
