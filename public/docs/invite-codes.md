# Invite codes


This service is in **early access** and access is **invite-only**.

Invites are created and distributed **only by admins** on our public **Discord server**.

## How It Works

* Join the Discord server
* Wait for an **invite wave**
* Each invite is **single-use**

## Rules

* Do not resell or automate claiming invites
* One account per person
* Abuse may result in an account termination
* also please don't spam our server begging for an invite

Public sign-ups may open once the platform is stable.
